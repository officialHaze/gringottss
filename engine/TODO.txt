============================== RULES ==================================
||  ** Write a task that needs to be done.                           ||
||     |                                                             ||  
||     --- Mark priority at the begining:-                           ||  
||          - 🟢 (Least priority)                                    ||  
||          - 🟡 (Medium priority)                                   ||  
||          - 🔴 (High priority)                                     ||  
||          - ⚫ (Critical priority)                                 ||
||  ** Maintain ONE line gap between each task.                      ||      
||  ** End every task with a fullstop/period.                        ||  
||  ** Always mark a task in-progress by adding ⌛ at the begining.  ||  
||  ** Always mark a task complete by adding ✅ at the begining.     ||
